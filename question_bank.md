# Daily Question Bank

**Goal**: Short (1–3 min), specific enough to spark real answers, varied to build a well-rounded achievement bank over time.  
**Usage**: Rotate categories daily or weekly. Aim for 1–2 questions per session.

## 1. Impact & Results
- What’s one thing you shipped, fixed, or improved today/this week?
- Did you make something faster, cheaper, or better? How? *(What was the before/after?)*
- What positive feedback did you get from a customer, colleague, or stakeholder?
- Did anything you worked on get used/measured this week? What were the numbers?
- What problem did you solve that others noticed?
- *What was the most valuable outcome from your work today? (e.g., saved time, money, happy client)*
- *How did your contribution affect the team, project, or company this week?*
- *What metric or result improved because of something you did?*

## 2. Leadership & Collaboration
- Did you help, mentor, or unblock someone today?
- Who did you collaborate with, and what came out of it?
- Did you lead a meeting, decision, or initiative?
- How did you handle a disagreement or tough conversation?
- Did you delegate or empower someone this week?
- *Who benefited from your guidance or support today? What was the result?*
- *What team goal did you advance through collaboration?*
- *Did you rally others around an idea or plan? How?*

## 3. Technical / Skill Growth
- What new tool, technique, or skill did you use or learn?
- Did you write code, design something, or analyze data today?
- What was the trickiest technical challenge you tackled?
- Did you automate or optimize something repetitive?
- *How much time/effort did your optimization or new skill save?*
- *What was the outcome of applying the new technique/skill?*
- *What resource (course, doc, colleague) helped you level up today?*

## 4. Initiative & Ownership
- What did you do without being asked?
- Did you spot an opportunity or risk that others missed?
- What experiment or idea did you push forward?
- Did you take ownership of something new?
- *What potential upside came from your proactive step?*
- *How did your initiative move the needle for the project/team?*
- *What would have happened if you hadn't stepped up?*

## 5. Communication & Influence
- Did you present, write, or explain something important?
- How did you influence a decision or get buy-in?
- Did you document, share knowledge, or onboard someone?
- *Who changed their mind or took action because of your input?*
- *What complex idea did you make clear to others today?*
- *How did your communication prevent a problem or create alignment?*

## 6. Reflection & Growth
- What surprised you this week?
- What would you do differently next time?
- What are you most proud of from the past few days?
- *What lesson from today will make you better tomorrow?*
- *How did today's work align with your long-term career goals?*
- *What strength did you lean on today?*

## Bonus / Context Questions (use occasionally)
- What was your main role/responsibility on this task/project? (helps AI tag correctly)
- Was this individual work, team effort, or cross-functional?

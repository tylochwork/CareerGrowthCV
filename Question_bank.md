# Daily Question Bank

Goal: Short (1–3 min), specific enough to spark real answers, varied to build a well-rounded achievement bank.

## Categories

### 1. Impact & Results
- What’s one thing you shipped, fixed, or improved today/this week?
- Did you make something faster, cheaper, or better? How?
- What positive feedback did you get from a customer, colleague, or stakeholder?
- Did anything you worked on get used/measured this week? What were the numbers?
- What problem did you solve that others noticed?

### 2. Leadership & Collaboration
- Did you help, mentor, or unblock someone today?
- Who did you collaborate with, and what came out of it?
- Did you lead a meeting, decision, or initiative?
- How did you handle a disagreement or tough conversation?
- Did you delegate or empower someone this week?

### 3. Technical / Skill Growth
- What new tool, technique, or skill did you use or learn?
- Did you write code, design something, or analyze data today?
- What was the trickiest technical challenge you tackled?
- Did you automate or optimize something repetitive?

### 4. Initiative & Ownership
- What did you do without being asked?
- Did you spot an opportunity or risk that others missed?
- What experiment or idea did you push forward?
- Did you take ownership of something new?

### 5. Communication & Influence
- Did you present, write, or explain something important?
- How did you influence a decision or get buy-in?
- Did you document, share knowledge, or onboard someone?

### 6. Reflection & Growth
- What surprised you this week?
- What would you do differently next time?
- What are you most proud of from the past few days?
